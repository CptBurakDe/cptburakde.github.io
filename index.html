About

Currently serving as an Oceangoing Watchkeeping Officer within tanker shipping companies, I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Maritime Transportation and Management Engineering from Karadeniz Technical University. My professional background encompasses a broad range of experience on Aframax, MR, LR, and Chemical/Oil tankers, where I have developed a comprehensive understanding of shipboard operations, navigation systems, and safety management under real-world conditions.

Over the course of my seagoing career, I have successfully participated in more than 300+ cargo operations and have been directly involved in over 20+ inspections, such as Flag State Control, SIRE, and Terminal Vettings. These experiences have solidified my practical expertise in regulatory compliance, cargo compatibility, safety standards, and operational efficiency, while fostering a detail-oriented and procedural approach to all aspects of tanker management.

My professional practice extends beyond technical proficiency to include a deep commitment to bridge resource management, leadership, and teamwork. Through close coordination with masters, chief officers, and shore-based management, I have contributed to safe navigation / passage planning, risk assessment, and the seamless execution of complex tanker operations. I maintain up-to-date knowledge of the IMO Publications such as IGC / IBC Codes, ISGOTT, and OCIMF guidelines, ensuring that all operations are carried out in full accordance with international maritime standards.

With a disciplined approach, I continue to build upon my maritime foundation by integrating technical expertise, situational awareness, and operational leadership. My goal is to further advance in the field of gas and chemical tanker operations, contributing to the industry’s evolving safety culture and technological innovation in maritime logistics.
