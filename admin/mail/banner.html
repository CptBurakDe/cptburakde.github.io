Make your own banner for your email signature

Create beautifully designed banners for your email signatures. Add them to your Email Signature Rescue signatures by uploading them to your default images folder (to use across all signatures), or directly to each signature in the images tab.

How to use our free email banner generator:

A simple guide to creating and uploading your own customized email banners to your Email Signature Rescue account.

  • Select from a background image, or paste your own image into the URL input box.
  • If you prefer a solid color background, uncheck the Use Background Image checkbox.
  • Change your overlay color to match your social icons and brand colors. You can choose the intensity on the slider.
  • Add your own CTA text and button text
  • Change colors, font sizes and button sizes.
  • Download the banner in your required size(s) by pressing Download next to each banner.
  • Download all banners in all sizes using the Download All Sizes button at the bottom.
  • Login to your Email Signature Rescue Dashboard and upload your banner images to your Default Images if you want to display the banners on all signatures, then edit the email signature and apply the Default Image in the Images tab. Or if you only want to use your banners on select email signatures, upload directly to the email signature by going to the Images tab and uploading it there.
